# Minor Acknowledgement of Risk Form

For Over 18's (parents/guardians) supervising Under 18's climbing at BoulderRyn.

## Flow
1. Video must be watched FIRST (YouTube embed: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-r2zbi21aks)
2. Cannot proceed to rest of form until video is complete
3. Fill in form details
4. Both supervisor AND dependent sign

## Differences from Adult Form
- Identical conditions of use, safety rules, bouldering rules, etc.
- Additional: **Under 18 Photo ID Consent** checkbox
- **Two signatures required**: Supervisee (parent/guardian) AND Dependent (the minor)
- **Minor Or Supervisee Details** section with "+ Add new" for multiple children
- Adult is the "Supervisor", children are "Dependents"

## Form Sections

### Conditions of Use
(Identical to Adult form — same full text for General Safety, Bouldering, Climbers Aged 8-17, Supervising, Warm Up Area, Photography, Matting Warning, Duty of Care, Unsupervised Climbing, Children rules)

### Climber Details
- Medical Conditions, if any* (text field)
- Level of climbing Experience?* (radio: New Climber / Climbed a few times / Regular Climber)
- Are you a member of any other walls? Which?* (text field)
- Roughly, how many times have you climbed in the past 12 months?* (text field)

### Confirmation Questions (all must be "yes")
1. Do you understand that failure to exercise due care, and follow the rules of the centre could result in injury or death?*
2. Have you watched the BoulderRyn Induction Video?*
3. Have you fully read and understood the conditions of this Participation Agreement?*
4. Do you agree to abide by the rules and conditions set out in this document when using the facilities at BoulderRyn?*
5. Do you understand that the matting does not remove the risk of injury?*
6. Having watched the video, do you feel you know how to climb safely?*
7. Are you, the person filling out this form, over 18 years of age?*
8. Do you understand that failure to exercise due care, and follow the rules of the centre could result in you being asked to leave BoulderRyn?*
