# BoulderRyn Gym Layout

## Walls

3 main walls:

1. **Cove Wall** (left side, teal/blue) — also serves as Competition Wall
2. **Mothership** (centre island, yellow/gold) — routes all the way around
3. **Magical Mystery** (right side, red/orange)

## Grading System

V-scale bouldering: VB (beginner), V0, V1, V2, V3, V4, V5, V6, V7, V8, V9+

## Route Colours

Routes are identified by hold colour + V grade:
- Black
- Yellow
- Green
- Purple
- Mint
- Red

Example: "Red V4 on Cove Wall"

## Layout Notes

- Mothership is a centre island with routes on all sides
- Cove Wall and Magical Mystery are on opposite sides
- There's an upper section/overhang area at the top of the Cove Wall
- The gym map is a bird's eye view looking down
